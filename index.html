Catch Phrase!




  • Determine how many players are on each team.
  • Designate a clue giver for each each team to handle the device
  • Each round is two minutes in length, with the number of rounds determined by the number of players.
  • Each clue master will be given a word to describe to their team, but can not say the word itself.
  • Once the word has been guessed correctly, quickly pass the device to the other clue master.
  • The last person to have the device when the timer goes off, loses the round and the other team gets a point.
  • Team with the most points after all rounds completed wins.
